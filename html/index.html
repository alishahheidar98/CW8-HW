What do you want to know about me?

My name is Ali Shahheidar. I was born in March 1381 in Ahvaz. Of course, we came to Tehran with my family when I was six months old. I had a good childhood. Since I was 7 years old, I became very interested in football and played in teams like Naft Tehran for several years. A few years later, by studying mathematics, I was able to enter the field of mechanical engineering at Sharif University of Technology. Of course, due to my interest in computer science, I changed my major to computer engineering in this university.

What did I do?

Let's take a look at my honors and achievements

If I want to explain to you from the beginning my entry into the real world, I can divide my honors into several categories, which include sports-educational and artistic ones. There is a lot to say in the field of sports, because I am basically an athlete and I have something to say in every branch of it. I have many city and country medals in football. In volleyball, I have the championship title of Tehran. I have the championship title in ping pong in Tehran schools. In the basketball branch, I have two championship and runner-up titles in Tehran schools. In the field of art, there is a lot to say, but the most striking one is the gold medal for wood carving and inlaid work in the whole country, which I won in the seventh, eighth and ninth years of middle school. And we get to education. Honestly, I didn't study much at first and I was looking for other jobs like football. Until one day, we made a deal with one of my friends. Whoever gets a higher grade in math is better than the other, and that's when I started studying. During several years, I won the title of runner-up and third in the city and country competitions in mathematics and physics, and until now I am studying computer engineering at Sharif University.

Olympiad

Again, I was accepted in the first stage and did not continue. I was very interested in participating in the Computer Olympiad, but time was lost, and I could not participate.
